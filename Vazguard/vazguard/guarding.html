Guarding both residential & commercial

Static guarding, is one of our expert fields, we can provide around the clock security for your premises, office blocks, industrial estates, factories, hospitals, schools, warehouses and building sites. All our security officers are fully trained, vetted and uniformed. They can be utilized as commercial officers to protect staff, stock and premises or front of house to assist your customers or staff. All our security officers are fully insured both at work and medical cover. Our approach is flexible, you may also want to use us as a backup facility, for when your existing staff are on holiday, sick or just as a temporary measure.
